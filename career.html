Elvis Presley

Elvis Aaron Presley was a legendary musician. Known mostly for his contribution to Rock n' Roll. He also sang blues and Gospel music.

List of skills/talents.

  • Gospel singer
  • Rock n' Roll singer
  • Played guitar
  • Dancer
  • Actor

List of published works

  • Jailhouse Rock
  • Teddy Bear
  • Hound Dog
  • That's Alright
  • and many more!

Places Elvis lived:

  • Tupelo MS, 1935-1948
  • Memphis, TN from 1948 till death on August 16, 1977 and is buried in Graceland.

Collaborators

  • Ben Weisman
  • Jerry Reed
  • Doc Pomus and Mort Shuman
  • Jerry Leiber and Mike Stoller
  • Mac Davis
  • Otis Blackwell

List of awards.

  • Multiple Gold Records
  • Multiple Grammys
  • Multiple Golden Boot awards.
